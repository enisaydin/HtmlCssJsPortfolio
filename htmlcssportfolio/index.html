About

Teddy Baldassarre is an American entrepreneur, watch enthusiast, commentator, and host of the world's leading YouTube channel for wrist-watch related content.

Teddy's videos engage over 2 million collectors every month around the world. From reviews to interviews with some of the most prominent collectors and watch executives, Teddy's content aims to educate and inspire the next generation of watch enthusiasts.
